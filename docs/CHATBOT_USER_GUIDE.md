# AI ChatBot - User Guide

## 👋 Welcome to the AI ChatBot

The AI ChatBot is your intelligent assistant within the NSRC AMS system. It can help you with questions about attendance, time logging, system features, and more.

## 🎯 How to Use

### Accessing the ChatBot

1. **Log in** to your NSRC AMS account
2. **Navigate** to the ChatBot page (usually in the main menu)
3. Or go directly to: `https://your-domain.com/chatbot`

### Sending a Message

1. **Type** your question in the message input box
2. **Click** the "Send" button or press Enter
3. **Wait** for the AI to respond (usually 1-3 seconds)
4. **Continue** the conversation naturally

### Example Questions

Here are some questions you can ask:

**About Attendance:**
- "What is the attendance policy?"
- "How do I mark myself present?"
- "What happens if I'm late?"

**About Time Logging:**
- "How do I log my time?"
- "Can I edit my time entries?"
- "What's the time-in/time-out process?"

**About the System:**
- "What features does this system have?"
- "How do I view my performance?"
- "Where can I find my attendance records?"

**General Help:**
- "How do I reset my password?"
- "Who do I contact for support?"
- "What are the system rules?"

## 🚀 Quick Actions

The ChatBot provides quick action buttons for common questions:

- **Attendance Policy** - Learn about attendance rules
- **Time Logging** - Get help with time tracking
- **System Features** - Explore available features

Just click any button to ask that question instantly.

## 🔄 Conversation History

The ChatBot remembers your conversation within the current session:

- **Previous messages** are shown in the chat window
- **Context is maintained** across multiple messages
- **You can ask follow-up questions** naturally
- **Refresh the page** to start a new conversation

### Example Conversation

```
You: What is the attendance policy?
AI: The attendance policy requires members to log in and out 
    for each session. Members are expected to arrive on time...

You: What if I'm late?
AI: If you're late, you can still log in, but it will be 
    recorded as a late entry. Repeated lateness may result in...

You: How late can I be?
AI: The system allows up to 15 minutes of grace period...
```

## 🆘 Troubleshooting

### ChatBot Not Responding?

**Problem**: I send a message but get no response
**Solution**:
1. Check your internet connection
2. Wait a few seconds (AI may be processing)
3. Try a simpler question
4. Refresh the page and try again

### Error Messages?

**"API key not configured"**
- The system administrator needs to set up the AI provider
- Contact your administrator

**"Rate limit exceeded"**
- You've sent too many messages too quickly
- Wait a few minutes and try again

**"Network error"**
- Check your internet connection
- Try again in a few moments

### Slow Responses?

**Problem**: The AI is taking a long time to respond
**Solution**:
1. Check your internet connection
2. Try a shorter, simpler question
3. The AI provider may be busy - try again later
